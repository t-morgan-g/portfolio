ZooPalooza

ZooPalooza is a Match 3 Puzzle Game created for my Honors Project at the University of Central Missouri. The goal of the game is to connect 3 or more the same type of animals to collect enough points to pass the level. Matching 4 or more animals results in one of three special animals depending on the direction the user ends in, while a connection of 8 or more results in a Unicorn special. Each special has a different effect when used within a connection.
